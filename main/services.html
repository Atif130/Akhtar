SERVICE

CCTV camera services play a crucial role in enhancing security and surveillance in both residential and commercial spaces. These services typically include the installation, maintenance, and repair of CCTV systems, ensuring that they operate efficiently to monitor activities in real time. Professional service providers offer a wide range of solutions tailored to meet specific needs, such as high-definition cameras, night vision capabilities, motion detection, and remote access through mobile apps. Regular maintenance is essential to keep the system functioning optimally, which involves cleaning lenses, checking connections, and updating software..

SALE

A sale is a transaction where goods, services, or property are exchanged for a specified amount of money or equivalent value. It typically involves a buyer and a seller, with the seller offering an item or service and the buyer agreeing to purchase it under mutually agreed terms. AMC

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is an agreement between a service provider and a customer for the regular maintenance and repair of products, equipment, or systems. The primary purpose of an AMC is to ensure the smooth functioning and longevity of the items under contract, such as machinery, appliances, IT systems, or infrastructure. Typically, an AMC outlines the scope of services, including preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, and emergency repairs, and may also cover the cost of spare parts and labor. The contract duration is usually one year, with the option for renewal.
