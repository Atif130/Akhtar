Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Akhtar CCTV Services, customer satisfaction is our utmost priority. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations and refunds. We aim to provide clarity and transparency in handling such requests to maintain trust and reliability.

1. Order Cancellations

Customers can request a cancellation of their orders under the following conditions:

  • Before Shipment: Orders can be canceled anytime before the product has been dispatched. No cancellation charges will apply.
  • After Shipment: Orders cannot be canceled once dispatched. However, customers can initiate a return upon receiving the product, subject to our return policy.

2. Refund Process

Refunds will be processed under the following circumstances:

  • Product Not Delivered: If a product is not delivered due to unforeseen circumstances, a full refund will be issued.
  • Product Defects: Refunds will be issued for defective products that cannot be replaced. Customers must report defects within 7 days of receiving the product.
  • Service Cancellation: If a service is canceled before commencement, a refund will be provided after deducting applicable charges.

3. Refund Timelines

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the request is approved. The amount will be credited to the original payment method or a bank account specified by the customer.

4. Conditions for Refund

Refunds will only be initiated under the following conditions:

  • The product is returned in its original condition with all accessories and packaging intact.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or receipt) is provided.
  • The refund request complies with the terms stated in this policy.
