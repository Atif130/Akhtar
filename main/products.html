WHAT WE PROVIDE

Attendance System

An attendance system is a tool designed to monitor, record, and manage the presence of individuals, typically in workplaces, educational institutions, or events.

Access Control System

An access control system is a security mechanism used to regulate who or what can view or use resources in a computing environment.

CCTV Camera

A CCTV camera is a surveillance device used for monitoring and recording activities in specific areas. It transmits video signals to a designated monitor.

Burglar Alarm System

A Burglar Alarm System is typically includes sensors for doors, windows, and motion detection, along with a control panel to manage the system

Fire Alarm System

A fire alarm system is a vital safety mechanism designed to detect and alert occupants of a building to the presence of fire, smoke, or carbon monoxide.

Smart Lock

Smart locks use various authentication methods, such as PIN codes, biometrics, or Bluetooth proximity, ensuring a balance between security and ease of use.

EPBAX

EPBAX serves as a central switching system for phone lines, connecting internal extensions to each other and to external telephone networks.

Video Door Phone

A Video Door Phone (VDP) is a security device that enables residents to see, speak with, and verify visitors before granting access.

Networking

Networking refers to the practice of connecting computers and devices to share resources, data, and communication. It is the backbone of modern communication.
